## üí° Key Recommendations

### Design Philosophy
1. **Start Simple** - High-level metrics first, drill down on demand
2. **Visual Hierarchy** - Most important KPIs prominently displayed
3. **Action-Oriented** - Every insight leads to actionable next step
4. **Explainable AI** - Don't just recommend, explain why

### Technical Approach
1. **Pre-calculate Aggregations** - Cache HEI scores and disparities
2. **Lazy Load Charts** - Only render visible tabs
3. **PHI Protection** - Automatic de-identification for exports
4. **Audit Everything** - Log all data access

### Business Value
1. **Compliance First** - Meet CMS HEI requirements
2. **Financial Impact** - Leverage 5% reward factor
3. **Quality Improvement** - Better outcomes for all members
4. **Strategic Advantage** - Data-driven equity optimization
