## 📋 Overview

**StarGuard Desktop** is a production-grade Medicare Advantage Intelligence Platform built for health plan analysts, Star Ratings program managers, and clinical operations leaders who need comprehensive, AI-assisted decision support — on a full desktop interface optimized for deep analytical work.

Powered by **Shiny for Python** and the **Anthropic Claude API**, StarGuard Desktop delivers 26 integrated analytical pages spanning HEDIS gap analysis, HCC risk stratification, Star Rating forecasting, ROI analytics, and AI-powered executive insights — all in a single unified platform.

This is not a dashboard. It is an intelligence system, designed by a practitioner who has lived inside these workflows across UPMC, Aetna, TriWest, and BCBS.

## 📊 Platform Modules (26 Pages)

### 🏥 HEDIS Gap Analysis
- Measure-level gap identification across all NCQA domains
- Member-level targeting with closure probability scoring
- HEDIS Portfolio Optimizer — priority ranking by impact and feasibility

### 🎯 HCC Risk Stratification
- Prospective HCC capture opportunity identification
- Risk score trending and RAF (Risk Adjustment Factor) forecasting
- Member cohort segmentation by risk tier

### ⭐ Star Rating Forecasting
- Multi-measure composite Star Rating simulation
- Threshold sensitivity analysis (critical cut-point modeling)
- Year-over-year trajectory modeling with intervention scenarios

### 💰 ROI Analytics
- Quality program investment modeling
- 2.8–4.1x ROI projection framework
- Cost-avoidance and revenue impact calculations

### 🤖 AI-Powered Executive Insights
- Natural language summaries of complex analytical outputs
- Board-ready narrative generation via Anthropic Claude API
- Strategic recommendation synthesis across all platform modules
