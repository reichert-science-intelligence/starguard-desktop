Suggested screenshots to showcase the **mobile Shiny** demo and compound engineering.

---

## 1. Hero: Dashboard + Mobile Ready badge

**What to capture:**  
Phone browser (or responsive desktop at ~375px width) showing:

- StarGuard AI header
- **📱 Mobile Ready** badge
- Four KPI cards (ROI, Star Rating, Members, Compliance)
- Start of HEDIS measure list or chart

“Production mobile Shiny for Python — HEDIS portfolio optimizer. iOS Safari & Android Chrome validated.”

---

## Tips

- Use **same Wi‑Fi** and `http://<your-ip>:8000` on phone for real-device screenshots.
- Prefer **portrait** for “mobile” and **landscape** only if you want to show chart width.
- Hide or blur any local IPs in public posts; for shinyapps.io use the public HTTPS URL.
- Keep **📱 Mobile Ready** badge visible in at least one screenshot.
