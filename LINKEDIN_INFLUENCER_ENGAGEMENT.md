## Engagement Best Practices

### 1. Timing
- **Engage within 24 hours** of influencer post
- **Early engagement** gets more visibility
- **Weekday mornings** (9-11 AM) for maximum reach

### 2. Tone
- **Professional but conversational**
- **Add value**, don't just promote
- **Ask thoughtful questions**
- **Share insights**, not just links

### 3. Content
- **Reference their post** specifically
- **Connect to your work** naturally
- **Provide context** before linking
- **Invite dialogue**, not just views

### 4. Follow-Up
- **Respond to replies** promptly
- **Engage with others** in the thread
- **Share their content** when relevant
- **Build relationships** over time

## Relationship Building

### Phase 1: Initial Engagement (Weeks 1-2)
- **Comment** on 2-3 posts per influencer
- **Follow** their content
- **Share** 1-2 relevant posts with commentary
- **Goal:** Get noticed, start dialogue

### Phase 2: Consistent Engagement (Weeks 3-4)
- **Continue commenting** on relevant posts
- **Respond** to their replies
- **Engage** with others in their threads
- **Goal:** Build familiarity, show expertise

### Phase 3: Relationship Development (Months 2-3)
- **Reference** their insights in your posts
- **Tag** them when relevant (sparingly)
- **Collaborate** on discussions
- **Goal:** Establish connection, mutual respect

### Phase 4: Ongoing Relationship (Months 4+)
- **Maintain** consistent engagement
- **Support** their initiatives
- **Collaborate** on content when appropriate
- **Goal:** Long-term professional relationship

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't: Over-Promote
> "Check out my project! [Link]"

### ✅ Do: Add Value First
> "This resonates with my work on [topic]. I've been addressing [challenge] through [approach]. [Link]. Would love your perspective."

---

### ❌ Don't: Generic Comments
> "Great post! Thanks for sharing."

### ✅ Do: Specific Insights
> "This aligns with my experience. I've found [specific insight] when implementing [solution]. [Link]. Curious about your thoughts."

---

### ❌ Don't: Link Dumping
> "[Link]"

### ✅ Do: Context Before Link
> "I've been working on [topic] that addresses [challenge]. Here's how: [Link]. Would appreciate your perspective."

---

### ❌ Don't: Ignore Replies
> [No response to influencer reply]

### ✅ Do: Engage in Dialogue
> "Thanks for the response! [Follow-up question or insight]. [Additional context if relevant]."
